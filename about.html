About Our Magical Pub

Located in the heart of Hogsmeade, The Three Broomsticks has been the favorite gathering place of witches, wizards, and students of Hogwarts School for centuries.

Known for its warm atmosphere and the legendary Butterbeer, the pub welcomes magical folk from all around the wizarding world.

A popular pub located in the wizarding village of Hogsmeade, The Three Broomsticks is a warm, cosy place owned and run by Madam Rosmerta. Established in 1452. Rumoured to be as old as the village itself, the inn was popular not only with villagers but also hags and other non-human magical beings. The Three Broomsticks served drinks such as Butterbeer and Firewhisky and was popular with Hogwarts teachers and students alike.
